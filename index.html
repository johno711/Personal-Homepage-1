About Me

Let me tell you a story....


  • Feb, 2016

    My Humble Beginning

    It was my final year of high school and I had no idea what I was going to. I was thinking about enrolling in business or science. But throughout my whole life, I knew I had always been working with computers....

  • Sept 2016

    My First Time

    Visual Basic was my very first language, I remember how hard it was, but eventually it got easy. I got the opportunity from a small courier business to build an invoice system, and I did it. That's when I figured it out....

  • Feb 2017

    RMIT University

    I had just enrolled at RMIT University into the Bachelor Of Computer Science, but that wasn't enough so I transferred into the Bachelor of Software Engineering. There I was able to start developing my skills.

  • July 2017

    Obstacle Avoidance Robot

    I bought myself an Arduino with one of those small starter kits. With a proximity sensor, wheels, motors and a chassis from ebay, I was able to program this car into avoiding walls and obstacles as it moved around.

  • Oct 2017

    Multiplayer Monster Game

    Worked on a Java team project for a multi-player pacman style game, where one monster hunts multiple players until one remains. Implemented Dijkstra's algorithm for the Monster Artificial Intelligence.

  • Feb 2018

    CSIT Mentor

    Grades were good so I got the oppurtunity to volunteer as a student mentor for Computer Science and Information Technology (CSIT). There I taught many students Java and C programmming as well as HTML5, CSS, PHP, JavaScript and MySQL.

  • March 2018

    My Genetic Algorithm

    I have a passion for artificial intelligence, so I took on guides and built a simple neural net in python as well as my own genetic algorithm in Processing as an attempt to learn AI. My smart dots can learn how to navigate a maze.

  • June 2018

    Front End Developer

    I started working as an intern at a melbourne based startup that offers solar comparisons on a web platform, I work here as a front end developer on the KeystoneJS website and have redesigned the google adwords campaign in HTML5.

  • August 2018

    Hackathon 2nd Place

    Me and some of my boys got together and joined the RMIT ITS hackathon. There we came up with a solution towards the theme of "flipped perspectives", aka "students teaching students". We came 2nd out of 13 teams with a $2000 AUD cash prize.

  • August 2018

    Hackathon 2nd Place Again

    2 weeks after our win, I joined the thingworx IoT/AR hackathon. Our team was developing a solution for the construction company LendLease, we solved their industry problem with augmented reality combined with a smart document manager. We were awarded the "Most Innovative Idea" by Hendry Group and were granted $4000 AUD as we came 2nd.
